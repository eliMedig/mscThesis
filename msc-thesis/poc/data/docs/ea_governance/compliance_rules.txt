Portfolio Compliance Rules (sample)

Compliance status: compliant, non_compliant, unknown.

Rules
- Every application MUST have a known compliance status. 'unknown' is treated as a
  data-quality defect and must be resolved.
- 'non_compliant' applications require a remediation plan with a target date.
- Critical applications that are 'non_compliant' are escalated to the architecture
  review board.

Data quality expectations
- Mandatory fields: app_id, app_name, owner, lifecycle_state, business_criticality,
  compliance_status, last_reviewed_date.
- Missing or 'unknown' values in mandatory fields are data-quality anomalies and
  are surfaced separately from statistical outliers.
